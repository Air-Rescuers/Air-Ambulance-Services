About Us

Air Rescuers World Wide Pvt Ltd. is a premier air ambulance service provider, renowned for delivering critical care and emergency medical transport across the globe. Established with a mission to offer swift, safe, and reliable medical evacuation, Air Rescuers has consistently set the standard in the industry with its unparalleled services and commitment to excellence.

Our fleet of state-of-the-art aircraft is equipped with advanced medical facilities, ensuring that patients receive the highest level of care during transit. Each flight is staffed with a team of highly trained medical professionals, including doctors, nurses, and paramedics, who specialize in emergency and critical care. This team is dedicated to providing comprehensive medical support from the moment of pickup to the final destination, ensuring patient safety and comfort throughout the journey.

Air Rescuers offers a wide range of services including emergency medical evacuation, patient transfer, organ transport, and specialized neonatal and pediatric transport. Our services are available 24/7, ensuring that help is always just a call away, no matter where you are.

In addition to our top-tier medical transport services, Air Rescuers is committed to customer satisfaction. Our dedicated customer support team works tirelessly to coordinate logistics, handle paperwork, and provide support to families and patients during challenging times. We understand the urgency and emotional stress associated with medical emergencies, and our goal is to make the process as smooth and stress-free as possible.

With years of experience and a track record of successful missions, Air Rescuers World Wide Pvt Ltd. stands as a trusted leader in the air ambulance industry, ready to respond swiftly and effectively to any medical emergency worldwide.
